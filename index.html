About Me

I’m a game developer who loves building systems that make games feel great to play. Thus far I have worked primarily in C++, C#, Unreal Engine, and Unity.

I’ve led small teams and built core systems like game state management, networking, and player-facing UI. I enjoy figuring out how everything connects under the hood just as much as making sure it feels smooth and intuitive on the surface.

Before game development, I worked in Medical Device Research, Technical Project Management in Healthcare, as well as Recruiting for start up game studios. All of that to say I’m comfortable owning systems, communicating across disciplines, and actually getting things done—not just prototyping ideas.

At the end of the day, I like building games that leave an impact on their player.
