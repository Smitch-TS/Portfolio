About Me

I’m a game developer focused on building systems that make games feel great—gameplay loops, UI, networking, and the glue that keeps everything steady under pressure. I work in C++, C#, Unreal, and Unity, and I’ve led small teams while shipping core systems like game state, multiplayer, and menus/HUD.

Before games, I worked in healthcare tech and recruiting for game startups, so I’m comfortable translating between disciplines and keeping projects moving. I like clean architecture, fast iteration, and a little bit of chaos—in the fun way.
